# Splash Loading Phrases

The intro splash picks a random phrase from this list and rotates through more
while the app finishes loading. Edit freely — one phrase per line.

Rules:
- Phrases must live under a `## ` section heading. Anything before the first
  `## ` heading is treated as documentation and ignored.
- Lines starting with `#` are ignored (headings/comments).
- Blank lines are ignored.
- Leading `-`, `*`, or `+` bullets are stripped.
- Do **not** include the trailing `...` — the app appends it.
