## Methodology
- Gate 1 (Contract Extraction): extracted lifecycle, capture, automation, flashback, persistence, and tooling contracts from both shards.
- Gate 2 (Journey Tracing): traced critical and destructive user journeys from launch through preview, recording, flashback, automation, and shutdown.
- Gate 3 (Failure Modes): simulated real-world timing, filesystem, resource pressure, long-run, and external-failure scenarios.
- Gate 4 (Contract Violations): compared callers against extracted contracts and identified state/error-handling disagreements.
- Gate 5 (Standard Review): performed conventional review and found concrete correctness, timeout, validation, and tooling bugs.
- Gate 6 (Self-Critique): deduplicated, downgraded, rejected false positives, and marked uncertain scenarios for investigation.
- Gate 7 (Synthesis): applied Gate 6 verdicts, ranked confirmed findings by user impact, and produced final fix-oriented report.
